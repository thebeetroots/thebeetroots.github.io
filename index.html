Our Story

The Beetroots formed in 2025 when three Toronto teens with diverse musical taste and background formed with aspirations of making an indie rock band. What started with aspirations of making music with friends has evolved into every teenager's dream of forming a band with their friends to create music.

Drawing inspiration from bands such as Slipknot, My Chemical Romance, Arctic Monkeys, and Radiohead, The Beetroots have crafted a distinctive sound that resonates with fans across the musical spectrum. Their name, a playful nod to their friend group's name, the Beetroot Group, perfectly encapsulates the sense of camaraderie the band has adopted towards its members and fans.

The Beetroots are committed to making great music, sticking to their independent, indie rock, and frankly emo, roots; creating music that excites the audience and pulls on your heartstrings. They are committed to authentic self-expression without judgement and showing the world their artistic visions.

The Beetroots are currently working on their debut EP, The Beetroots are excited to share their music with a wider audience. The Beetroots is all about creating music that excites crowds, pulls on your heartstrings, and frankly, music that you can love!
