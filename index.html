Our Story

March 2025

The Beginning

Three Toronto teens with diverse musical backgrounds came together with a shared dream: to create an indie rock band.

"Did you know beetroots are vegetables? They're also a band."
March 2025

Finding Our Identity

Drawing inspiration from bands like Slipknot, My Chemical Romance, Arctic Monkeys, and Radiohead, we began crafting our distinctive sound that would resonate with fans across the musical spectrum.

Our name, a playful nod to our friend group's name, the Beetroot Group, perfectly encapsulated the sense of camaraderie we wanted to foster.

April 2025

Figuring Out Our Instruments Situation

We're still figuring out instruments. We still require an electric guitar & amp, and hopefully a microphone and/or drum amp. We've been running off of spare cash and birthdays to get instruments so far.

"We'll figure out our instrument situation."
April 2025

Recording & Covers

Although we're still figuring out our instruments issue, we have a long list of songs we'd like to cover and musical ideas that could excite the world.

We're committed to making great music, sticking to our independent, indie rock, and frankly emo, roots; creating music that excites the audience and pulls on your heartstrings.

The Future

Looking to the Future

We're currently working on our debut EP and excited to share our music with a wider audience.

"We're excited for our music, and we're ready to make our musical ideas into songs."
