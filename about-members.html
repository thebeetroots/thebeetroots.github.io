The Beetroots is the musical brainchild of Tessa, Alisha, and Kai, the three founding members of the Beetroots. The Beetroots is dedicated to crafting a distinctive independent sound that allows the Beetroots to fully express their music.

Our music is like no other, with members from marching, operatic, choir, and other diverse musical backgrounds. Our band remains independent to be able to experiment with the boundless limits of musical creation..

Through our music, we create an experience, not just a song. An experience that traverses through mindscapes, challenging your previous thoughts on what music was. We are like no other.
